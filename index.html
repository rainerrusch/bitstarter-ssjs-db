FAQ

Why automate my garden?
Many people like to have a nice and well-maintained garden, but few have time to actually maintain one.
Most importantly, a garden needs regular watering, especially in hot weather. Dragging out hoses and getting everything watered takes a significant amount of time. Additionally, how do you know when the soil requires watering, or when it has sufficient water? Water is becoming a scarce resource in many area's, wasting water by watering too much should also be avoided.
eGarden assist's you with maintaining a growing Garden, providing water when needed, without wasting water.
For which environments can I use eGarden?
eGardener can be used for virtually any garden environment since it provides a scalable architecture. Additionally, eGardener can easily be customized or extended by the community.
Which components will you use?
The eGarden controller(s) are composed of Arduino boards (http://www.arduino.cc/).
Communication is handled by XBee Wireless RF modules (http://www.digi.com/xbee/).
Sensors and Actuators are sourced from reputable supplies, keeping in mind that these components should be available world-wide.
What else do I need?
eGardener provides sensors, actuators, controllers and software needed to automate a garden.
Additionally you will need pipes, connectors and dispensing units as per your requirement. These are freely available in any local home and garden supplies store.
Why invest in an Open Source solution?
Designing and building any solution requires a significant amount of time for research, implementing, testing and maintaining.
Open Source solutions cannot exist without community support, be it technical or financial, and if such support is not provided, any project is set for failure, or at a minimum, further developments and enhancements will not happen.
By contributing to eGardener you ensure that this project will not disappear into the nirvana of the webs.
